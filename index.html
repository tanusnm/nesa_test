About Us

NESA Innovation Labs is a technology consultancy company that specializes in providing engineering project management, systems engineering, and solutions to challenging problems for tech startups in India and abroad.


Co-founded by Sanjay Patel and Neha Patel, our mission is to solve core technical challenges and provide innovative solutions to a wide range of customers across industries. With a unique skill set and position in the industry, we strive to cater to potential customers and help them take their businesses to the next level.

Our Services

At NESA Innovation Labs, we offer a range of services
to help startups and companies tackle their technical challenges and achieve their goals.

Our services include:

Engineering Project Management

We provide expert guidance and management of engineering projects, ensuring timely delivery, cost-effectiveness, and quality.

Systems
Engineering

We help design and develop complex systems, ensuring integration and compatibility across various components and subsystems.

Mechatronics and Control System Development

Our team specializes in mechatronic product development, including hardware design, software development, modeling, simulation, and control system implementation.

Model-Based Design

We leverage tools like MATLAB, Simulink, and Stateflow to create models, perform simulations, and generate code for rapid prototyping and system verification.

ISO 26262 Compliance

We offer services related to ISO 26262 functional safety standard, including hazard analysis, risk assessment, and ASIL allocation.

Consulting and Coaching

We provide valuable advice, mentoring, and coaching to startup teams, helping them navigate technical challenges and make informed decisions.


Feel free to reach out to us to discuss your specific requirements, deliverables, and timelines.
